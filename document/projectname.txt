HireNowPro — Short description

Elevator pitch

HireNowPro is an AI‑powered recruitment platform that automates candidate verification, runs asynchronous video interviews, and delivers objective, data‑driven pass/fail recommendations so HR teams hire faster and more fairly.
One‑paragraph product description

HireNowPro streamlines pre‑hire screening by combining reapplication verification, automated video responses, and AI/ML analysis (RAG, embedding search, LangChain‑style orchestration). Submissions enter an asynchronous processing queue, receive standardized scoring and insights, and trigger configurable notifications and HR portal updates—reducing time‑to‑hire, improving consistency, and preserving audit trails for compliance.
Key features (concise)

Reapplication & identity verification
Asynchronous AI‑scored video interviews
Explainable scoring + pass/fail outcomes
Processing queue with scalable async workers
HR portal dashboard + applicant notifications
Integrations: ATS, email, calendar, SSO
Data security, audit logs, and bias‑mitigation controls
Primary benefits

Faster screening at scale
Consistent, objective candidate evaluation
Reduced manual workload for recruiters
Clear auditability for compliance and fairness
